BORDER COLLIE

Border Collies are the most smartest dogs in the world according to research published in the book "The Intelligence of Dogs" by psychologist Stanley Coren in 1944.

  • Weight: 12-20 kg.
  • Height: 46-56 cm.
  • Life Span: 10-17 years, avarage of 12 years.
  • Coat: smooth or rough double coat with colors like black and white, red, shaded sable, and others.

The Border Collies are very employed in activities like herding livestock, especially sheap, because of their intelligence and obedience. They have a demanding personality and need mental stimulation and exercises every day. They can also recognize words and objects being able to identify which group a particular object belongs to.
